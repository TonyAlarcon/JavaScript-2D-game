The Game Project

Directions: Press "Up", "Down", "Left", or "Right" arrow keyboards to move the player. Goal is for the player to reach the top of the arena while avoiding all obstacles. You begin with 3 lives. Game is over after you have exhausted all your lives. Default difficulty level is medium. Click on "Start/Resume" button to begin playing.

Good luck.
